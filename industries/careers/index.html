Flexible Schedule

Flexible work schedules are increasingly becoming common in the workforce. With an aim to provide job satisfaction and achieve high productivity, we give a complete freedom to our employees to work in flexible schedules. This ultimately encourages employees to be creative and content at our workplace.

Compititive Salary

We value our employees and it is very important to keep rewarding the workforce based on their performances. We, at Tagbin, work with a competency-based pay structure to motivate our employees and maintain a healthy balance between job satisfaction and monetary benefits.

New Challenges

Nothing great can be achieved without a few bottlenecks on the way and therefore, our challenging work environment always pushes our employees to achieve something greater. This nurtures them professionally and personally as well.

Peaceful Work Environment

A peaceful environment adds a feeling of tranquillity and security to the workplace. We, at Tagbin, believe in a friendly, healthy and peaceful environment. Apart from working hard towards a goal, we also shed light on important aspects of a work culture.

Business Development Head

Job Description

Department: Sales
Location: Gurgaon
Travel Required: Yes
Roles and Responsibility
  • Ability to lead business development initiatives including responding to RFPs, preparation and delivery of client presentations with the objective of sales and business development. Experience of working on government RFPs is a plus.
  • Manage the end-to-end sales process for all opportunities including initial client communication, on-site presentations, RFI response, multi-day client workshops, RFP submission, negotiation and deal signing. The candidate is the focal point for all communication and sales activities with prospects and customers.
  • Provide support to customers during initial phases of an engagement. Follow up and ensure total client satisfaction through the life cycle of the relationship.
  • Conceptualize different solutions on the basis of client or projects.
  • Demonstrate strong personal communication and presentation skills to establish interest, credibility and trust.
  • Working at the stage of pre sales till closer to the deal.
  • Ability to manage cross functional teams and mentor junior team member's.
  • Conceptual thinking and ability to find innovative ways to solve analytical problems.
  • Working with multiple stakeholders and assisting them in technology related solutions.
  • Help define solutions that are relevant to the practice and the sector.
  • Drive business development by sourcing leads and owning pursuits end to end.
  • Own and manage the project deliveries by successfully managing client engagements and internal team as per defined timelines.
  • Drive the solution thinking within the project team.
  • Mine existing clients for additional business opportunities and drive aggressive sales efforts.
  • Ensure project economics by managing invoicing, collections and managing project expenses with the team.
  • Drive Knowledge Management- mentor & develop managers.
  • Personally develop strong, long-term relationships and referrals with senior management at targeted firms.
Experience background:
  • 5+ years of experience with Govt. sector (IT Solution).
  • Experience in working with C level executive clients.
Qualifications and Education Requirements:
  • MBA from a top - tier business school

Tender Executive

Job Description

Department: Operations
Location: Gurgaon
Travel Required: No
Roles and Responsibility
  • Identify and Evaluate tenders for potential application by the company post thorough scanning. Handling or working on GEM Portal (Govt eMarket), E-procurement & E-Tender Portal.
  • Online registration on the various portal for tender.
  • Searching for the right tender, RFP or Bid from different tender portals.
  • Preparing & uploading the tender documents online & final submission of tender or bid.
  • Monitor the tender application and filing in order to ensure it is submitted duly along with all required formalities.
  • Ensure all completed tenders of the company are well documented in a timely manner with appropriate level of detail.
  • Analyse problem areas for the company and suggest appropriate solutions for the same, in consultation with the senior management.
  • Attend Pre-Tender meetings on behalf of the company, wherever necessary, and make official representations.
  • Candidates should be well versed with Indian Contracts Act and Arbitration Act.
Skills Required:
  • Experience in the service industry is desirable.
  • Should be extremely conversant with online tendering portals, key tender jargons and filing process.
  • Strong time-management and organisational skills.
  • Ability to coordinate several tasks at the same time.
  • Well versed with Microsoft Office Softwares.
Qualifications and Education Requirements:
  • Any Graduate
  • 3 + years
