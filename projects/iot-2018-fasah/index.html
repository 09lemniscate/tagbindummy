People Get Acquainted with a the Brand in an Interactive Way

This event was organized to help people notice a brand and understand its value. A high end ‘Interactive Projection Wall’ offered information in an engaging way with an effective use of graphics.

The Experiential Centre built by Tagbin provided information about brands with infographics, as well as possibilities for innovation of the products. Doodle maps and wall graffiti was used to make the exercise visually and aesthetically appealing for the visitors.
